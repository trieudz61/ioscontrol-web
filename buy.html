Frequently Asked Questions
Can I use the same key on multiple devices?
No. Each license key is bound to one device's hardware UDID. Each device needs its own license.
What if I reinstall the tweak?
Your license is automatically restored! The system recognizes your device's UDID and re-activates the license — no need to re-enter the key.
Can I extend my existing license?
Yes! If you buy additional days while your current license is still active, the new days are added to your remaining time. For example: 23 days remaining + 7-day purchase = 30 days total.
What happens when my license expires?
The app reverts to Free mode with a 2-minute script limit. All your scripts and data are preserved — just purchase again to continue.
Is the payment secure?
Yes! Payment is processed via PayOS with bank-level security. We never store your banking details.
